About The Language of Letting Go

Discover inspiration & daily encouragement with this accessible site, offering The Language of Letting Go — Melody Beattie's meditations on codependency, recovery, self-care, boundaries, and the steady practice of letting life unfold.

The meditation text is by Melody Beattie. This web companion was built to make the readings easier to revisit online; the underlying writing remains the work of its original author and publisher.

The book The Language of Letting Go can be purchased from Amazon.
