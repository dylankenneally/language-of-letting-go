title: Letting Go of Urgency
---

One thing at a time. That's all we have to do. Not two things at once, but one thing done in peace. One task at a time. One feeling at a time. One day at a time. One problem at a time. One step at a time.

*One pleasure at a time.*

Relax. Let go of urgency. Begin calmly now. Take one thing at a time. See how everything works out?

{% focal %}
Today, I will peacefully approach one thing at a time. When in doubt, I will take first things first.
{% endfocal %}
