displayDate: June 14
title: Letting Go of Timing
---

<span class="quote">
When the time is right, child. When the time is right. How often have we heard
</span>

those words—from a friend, a sponsor, our Higher Power? We want things so badly—that job, that check, a relationship, a possession. We want our life to change. So we wait, sometimes patiently, sometimes anxiously, wondering all the while: When will the future bring me what I long for? Will I be happy then? We try to predict, circling dates on the calendar, asking questions. We forget that we don't hold the answers. The answers come from God. If we listen closely,

*well hear them. When the time is right, child. When the time is right. Be happy now.*

<span class="focal-point">
Today, I will relax. I am being prepared. I can let go of timing. I can stop manipulating outcomes. Good things will happen when the time is right, and they will happen naturally.
</span>
